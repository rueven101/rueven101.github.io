Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2025


1. Overview

BellyBelles Coffee values your trust and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your data when you visit our website or café, place orders, or interact with us.


2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and contact details (email, phone, address)
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party payment processors)
  • Order history and customer preferences
  • Device information (browser type, IP address, etc.)
  • Feedback or reviews you submit

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process orders and payments
  • Respond to customer inquiries
  • Improve our products, services, and website
  • Send promotional offers (only if you've opted in)

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share your information with trusted service providers when necessary for operations (e.g., payment gateways, delivery services), and only under strict confidentiality agreements.


5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.


6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. While no system is 100% secure, we work hard to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.


7. Your Rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time

To make a request, please contact us at support@bellybellescoffee.com.


8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we learn that we have, we’ll delete it promptly.


9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We’ll post the new version on this page and revise the effective date above. Continued use of our services means you agree to the updated terms.


10. Contact Us
